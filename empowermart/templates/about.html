About Us

Get to know us more!

Welcome to EmpowerMart, a platform created by IGDTUW students to empower our college community through innovation and entrepreneurship. Founded by Anusha Garg, Lavanya Manocha, and Mishthi Sachdeva, B.Tech AIML 2nd-year students, EmpowerMart connects students, entrepreneurs, alumni, and career aspirants. By showcasing student-run businesses and projects, we aim to foster support, encourage local entrepreneurship, and drive sustainable growth. Whether you're an aspiring entrepreneur, a student exploring the marketplace, or someone passionate about supporting local talent, EmpowerMart connects you to valuable opportunities within the IGDTUW community.

EmpowerMart is more than just a platform for buying and selling; it is a bridge connecting students to their entrepreneurial peers, allowing businesses to showcase their products and services to a broad college audience. We believe in amplifying the voices of student entrepreneurs and helping them reach new heights within the supportive environment of IGDTUW. Our platform is tailored to meet the needs of students looking for a personalized marketplace, where they can discover, connect, and directly support their fellow students. Additionally, by highlighting the diverse range of businesses on our platform, we provide visibility to ventures that may not have had a chance to reach an audience otherwise. EmpowerMart is designed to grow with the ambitions of our community, ensuring that every student entrepreneur has an opportunity to shine and find support among their peers.

Alongside student businesses, EmpowerMart proudly celebrates the journeys of our inspiring alumni who have successfully launched their own startups. We offer a dedicated section for alumni insights, sharing their stories of resilience, innovation, and growth to inspire current students and guide them toward potential career paths. Our alumni stories serve as both motivation and networking opportunities, helping students understand the challenges and rewards of entrepreneurship. In addition, we curate exclusive job and internship listings, providing students with direct access to career prospects from within our network of alumni startups and other trusted sources.
